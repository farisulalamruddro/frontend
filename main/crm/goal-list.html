About Goals

  • Goals help your team achieve their defined Salves Targets.
  • You can assign individual Goals to your Sales team where you define the number of expected deals advanced /won within a certain amount of time.
  • Personal Goals can be tracked from pie charts in pipeline view of Deals page, and the overall view can be tracked from this page.
  • All the Goals will be carried over to next period.
  • Metrics by which you can define Goals:
    • A) Deals Advanced
    • B) Deals Won you can track Value and Quantity of deals using these metrices.
  • Know More

Example

  • If you define a Goal for A say A should win 10 deals a month. Whenever A wins a deal. A's goal progress will be updated. This progress can be tracked from tiny Pie charts in Pipeline view and in Goals page.
  • When a month ends. A's Goal target will automatically set to 10 deals regardless of last month’s won deals.
